# Seer Comms Telecom Operations Intelligence LiveStack Guide

## Introduction

Telecommunications providers are under pressure to keep subscribers connected while network demand, care signals, field capacity, service orders, and churn risk move at different speeds. Network operations teams need to see where demand pressure is building. Care leaders need to know which subscriber signals deserve priority. Field teams need regional capacity and dispatch visibility. Retention teams need to understand revenue and churn exposure before a service issue becomes an account loss. Data teams need these workflows to stay governed, explainable, and connected to operational systems.

This runbook supports the Seer Comms Telecom Operations Intelligence LiveStack Demo. The demo shows how Oracle AI Database 26ai can help communications service providers bring those workloads together on one connected data foundation. Instead of splitting relational service orders, JSON documents, graph relationships, spatial network context, vector search, machine learning, natural-language SQL, and AI-assisted action logging across separate systems, the LiveStack shows how those capabilities can work against the same governed Oracle data model.

In the demo, Seer Comms uses Oracle AI Database to connect mobile service demand, subscriber signals, network impact relationships, field capacity, service orders, predictive assurance, conversational data access, and AI-assisted interventions. The demo follows a **South Florida 5G mobile demand surge** as the hero scenario: subscriber signals appear, graph and spatial views explain who and what is affected, service orders and field dispatches show the operational response, predictive models quantify churn and revenue exposure, and AI-assisted workflows recommend governed next actions.

Estimated Demo Time: 90 minutes

Each scene is designed to take between 5 and 10 minutes.

### Objectives

In this LiveStack Demo, you will:
- Explore the key telecom use cases featured in the Seer Comms demo, from data foundation and operations-center visibility to subscriber signals, network impact, field capacity, service orders, predictive assurance, conversational data access, and AI-assisted service assurance.
- Follow the South Florida 5G demand-surge story across the eight active telecom use cases: detecting service pressure, prioritizing subscriber signals, investigating impact, locating capacity constraints, acting on service orders, predicting churn and revenue exposure, asking live data questions, and guiding governed interventions.
- Understand how common telecom challenges such as fragmented OSS/BSS data, service assurance pressure, subscriber churn, capacity exposure, field dispatch complexity, limited self-service analytics, and governed AI adoption are addressed in the demo flow.
- See how Oracle AI Database 26ai supports each use case with converged capabilities including relational data, JSON, graph, spatial, vector search, machine learning, natural-language SQL, and AI-assisted operations.
- Connect each scene to a practical business outcome, so the demo shows not only what the application does, but why the Oracle data platform matters for modern communications providers.
